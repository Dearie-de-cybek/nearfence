How It Works

Four Steps to Verified Attendance

From login to clock-in, every action is GPS-confirmed and audit-ready — no paper, no guesswork, no fraud.

STEP 01

Secure Login

Sign in with your verified employee account. Role-based access ensures only authorised users can begin a shift.

STEP 02

Arrive at Worksite

Head to your assigned location. Each worksite has a pre-configured geofence that defines the valid clock-in boundary.

STEP 03

GPS Verified

NearFence compares your live GPS coordinates against the approved boundary. Outside the fence — clock-in is blocked.

STEP 04

Clocked In & Tracked

Attendance is recorded with a verified timestamp. Managers see it instantly on the live dashboard — zero manual input.
