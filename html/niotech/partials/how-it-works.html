How NearFence Works

STEP - 01
Secure Login
Sign in with your verified employee account to start your shift.
STEP - 02
Arrive at Worksite
Head to your assigned location — the approved geofenced site for your shift.
STEP - 03
Location Verified
NearFence checks your GPS position against the worksite boundary automatically.
STEP - 04
Clock In and Track
Clock in within the geofence and your attendance is recorded and timestamped.
