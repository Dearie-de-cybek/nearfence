Problem

The Attendance Problem

Without location verification, attendance records are built on trust alone, leaving payroll, compliance, and workforce planning exposed.

Limited Workforce Visibility

Managers can't confirm who's actually onsite or when a shift truly began.

Time Fraud and Buddy Punching

Manual and remote check-ins make it easy to clock in for someone else or from off-site.

Audit and Compliance Gaps

Without location-backed records, attendance data can't withstand a payroll or compliance audit.
